Our Story

HS Shivania Group was founded with a vision to provide comprehensive solutions for both household cleaning needs and industrial requirements. What started as a small venture has grown into a trusted name in the industry.

Today, we operate through two specialized divisions: Shivania Group focusing on premium cleaning products for homes and offices, and HS Shivania Group providing industrial-grade equipment and supplies for manufacturing and commercial sectors.
